Why Accessibility is Important

Equal Access for All

To make the website easily accessible for users with challenges or disabilities.

Legal and Regulatory Compliance

Many countries have enacted laws and regulations that mandate digital accessibility. Failing to comply with these regulations can lead to legal consequences, fines, and reputational damage.

Improved User Experience

Accessible design often leads to better user experiences for everyone. Clear navigation, well-structured content, and user-friendly interfaces benefit all users, regardless of ability.

Resulting Business Benefits for Accessibility

By ensuring your digital content is accessible, you can reach a wider audience, including those with disabilities. This can lead to increased traffic, a larger market share, and potential business growth.

Increased Revenue

An accessible application can lead to increased conversions, as users have a better experience navigating and interacting with your products or services. Higher user engagement often translates to higher revenue.

Improved Customer Satisfaction

Creating accessible websites and applications improves user experience, satisfaction, and brand perception.

Government and Corporate Contracts

Meeting accessibility standards is a requirement for many governments and corporations. This positions your business as an attractive partner for contracts and collaborations.

Enhanced Brand Reputation

Mitigation of Legal and Reputational Risks

Complying with accessibility regulations and guidelines reduces the risk of legal action and potential fines. Avoiding accessibility-related lawsuits and negative publicity protects your brand's reputation.

Which organizations consider accessibility to be important?

Ensuring equal access and usability for all individuals, regardless of their abilities, is fundamental in digital design and development. Here are some types of organizations and applications that should prioritize accessibility and why.

Government and Public Services domain applications

Citizens use government websites and apps for information and services. Accessible platforms guarantee that everyone can access essential information, apply for services, and engage with public resources. Accessibility also helps governments follow legal requirements, such as the Section 508 standards in the US.

Educational Institutions domain applications

Educational websites and e-learning platforms must be accessible to provide equal learning opportunities for students with disabilities. Accessible educational materials ensure that all students can access course content, participate in discussions, and take assessments without any barriers.

E-Commerce and Retail domain applications

E-commerce websites must be accessible to all customers, including those with disabilities. This includes browsing, purchasing, and customer support. Accessibility improves user experience, leading to increased sales and loyalty.

Healthcare and Medical Services domain applications

Medical information websites, patient portals, and healthcare applications need to be accessible so that patients with disabilities can access their medical records, schedule their appointments, and obtain relevant health information especially in emergency situations.

News and Media Outlets domain Applications

It is important for news websites, blogs, and media platforms to prioritize accessibility so that everyone can access news and information. This also aligns with ethical journalism principles by reaching a broader audience.

Financial Services domain applications

Individuals use financial websites and apps to manage finances, pay bills, and access account info. Accessibility ensures that people with disabilities can manage their money independently, improving their financial inclusion.

Social Media and Communication

Social media and communication tools should be accessible for people with disabilities to connect, share, and participate in online conversations. Inclusive platforms promote community engagement and prevent isolation.

Employment and Job Searching domain applications

Individuals with disabilities should have access to job boards, career websites, and employment applications to search for jobs, apply, and access employment resources. This promotes equal opportunities for all job seekers.

Entertainment and Streaming Services

Individuals with disabilities should have access to entertainment, video streaming, and gaming platforms for recreational activities and enjoyment of multimedia content.
